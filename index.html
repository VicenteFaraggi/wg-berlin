About Me...

A little about me!...

Hey I'm Vicente, 33 years old. I've been here in Berlin for 5 years now. Unfortunately the landlord of my actual WG didn't want to renew our contract due to some flat renovation. So here I'am, one more time with all the energy looking for a new place to call home.

01. Introduction

I'm originally from Chile, but left my country 5 years ago to start a new adventure and create my very best version of myself and also to epxpand my carrer path in Europe.

02. Professional Path

I have two degrees, the first one in Information System and Data Analyst and the second one in Gastronomy. Yes, for strange that sound, I have a very eclectic taste. I have worked 3 years as a Data Analyst and a little more than 3 years in Gastronomy.

03. WG Experience

Since I came to Berlin 5 years ago, I have lived in 3 WG plus one single room apartment.I know how to adapt to new places and also I'm Very respectful with each personal space. I like to maintain the common areas tidy and clean as well as respect the schedules.

04. Body Movement

A couple of years ago I started exploring with my body, focusing mainly on some yoga and basic calisthenics trying to achieve a handstand. I also play with breathing exercises sometimes.

05. Hobbies & Free Time

In my free time I like to explore my creativity. I do some art like acrylic painting and collage. Also I'm learning about coding art, mixing two of my favourite activities, coding and creating art. I also enjoy riding my bike every day, listening to music and watching a good movie. 

06. WG Atmosphere

I'm looking for a new place to call home. I would like a place where we can create a nice atmosphere, maybe dinner some time and have some quality vibe in the flat. I have always thought that your house environment is crucial for your everyday life. So I respect and try to create the best energy possible
